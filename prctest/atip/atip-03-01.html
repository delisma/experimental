ATIP request service

You can request information about the government or information about a person with the Access to Information and Privacy Request Service.

Your request will be sent to the institution you select. Institutions are required to respond to requests within 30 days by providing records or explaining why more time is needed. An institution’s access to information and privacy officer will contact you directly if your request is incomplete or requires clarification.

Request information about the government

You can request general records about the operations of government institutions under the Access to Information Act.

Examples include records with information about:

  • government spending
  • programs
  • policies
  • government activities

Submission time: 5 mins

Fee: $5:00 (VISA, MasterCard or American Express are accepted. Payments cannot be made through debit or prepaid credit cards)

Request information about a person

You can request personal information about individuals held by government institutions for the purpose of administering programs and services under the Privacy Act.

Examples include records that have:

  • your personal information
  • information about an individual (consent required)
  • information on behalf of a deceased individual
  • historical information about an individual (such as war records)

Submission time: 5 mins

Fee: None
